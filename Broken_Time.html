Broken Time

Broken Time is a game made by Mostafa M. Soud (Game Designer and Programmer) and Haider (Artist and Designer)

The game includes 3 levels and a final boss fight , Having various mechanics to finish the levels ,

The main mechanic of the game is going back in time which can save you from various attacks and fails.
